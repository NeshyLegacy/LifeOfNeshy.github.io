Big Top Blast

This project was created in 2021 with AIT with an Internship with Liminal VR. Our Group consisted of Scott Alec and I being the programmers and Level Designers, Jessica Fredricksen as the 3D Artist and Vasilios Moutafis as our mentor.
Liminal VR is a platform consisting of various different experiences ranging from Calm and pain relief to Energise and Awe. We wanted to create our project under the Energise category.

If our project met the company's standards and and requirements and is overall polished, they would publish our project on their platform, which became the groups number one goal and we ended up succeeding in that goal and our project can be played right now!
